Jack Flash Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

Introduction

Jack Flash ("we," "our," or "us") operates a mobile application that allows customers to order products from our convenience store locations for pickup. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth (for age-restricted product verification)

Order Information

  • Purchase history
  • Product preferences
  • Pickup location and time preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

Age Verification

  • For tobacco and alcohol purchases, we collect and verify age using Stripe Identity
  • We store only your age verification status (21+), not your full date of birth
  • ID verification data is processed by Stripe and not stored on our servers
  • Store employees may manually verify ID for in-person purchases

Location Data

  • We collect your location to show nearby store locations
  • This data is used only to improve your experience and is not shared with third parties

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Verify age for restricted product purchases
  • Send order notifications and updates
  • Manage your loyalty points and rewards
  • Improve our services and app functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe: For payment processing and age verification
  • Supabase: Our secure database provider
  • Legal authorities: When required by law

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. Payment data is processed through Stripe's PCI-compliant systems. Age verification is conducted through Stripe Identity with bank-level security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Update your information at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contactus@wortmanstores.com

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Age-Restricted Products

Important: Purchasing tobacco or alcohol products requires age verification. You must be 21 years or older. Even with app verification, you must present valid government-issued photo ID at pickup for all age-restricted purchases. Store employees reserve the right to refuse sale if ID does not match or appears invalid.

Loyalty Program

Our Kickback Points loyalty program tracks your purchases to award points. Points have no cash value and cannot be transferred. We reserve the right to modify or terminate the loyalty program at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain certain information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of your information (note: we do not sell personal information).
